## How it learns from you

This isn't a static quiz. "Am I Cooked?" uses **Active Learning**.

If the AI predicts you're "Cooked" but you ace your exam, you can prove it wrong. The system accepts your feedback ("I actually got a 95!") and retrains itself. Over time, it learns that maybe *you* are the type of student who can pull an all-nighter and still succeed, creating a smarter, personalized predictor for everyone.

## Why use it?

*   **Identify Blind Spots:** You might think skipping one class is fine, but the model might show it's your biggest risk factor.
*   **Visualize Your Data:** See clear trends in how your sleep or social life correlates with your academic performance.
*   **Get Peace of Mind:** sometimes you just need a robot to tell you that you're going to be okay.
